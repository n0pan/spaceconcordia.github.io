SPACE CONCORDIA IS a student society in the Engineering faculty at Concordia University, composed of undergraduate and graduate students, that promotes Space and space engineering in Concordia and Montreal through projects, presentations within the University and outreach programs. It is made up of three divisions: Spacecraft, Rocketry, and Robotics.

IF you are interested in working on projects which will pit your engineering skills against some of the most challenging environmental conditions, most extreme speeds, and the most complex missions of the final frontier, then this is the society for you!
