This year, for the first time EVER, Space Concordia will be hosting its very first SCAVENGER HUNT! You, my fellow minions, will go on a quest to follow your superior, a powerful villain. Your teams' goal: get as many points possible! The team with the most points not only gets a prize, but glory over all the other teams. Tickets will be $10.00 for ENG students and $15.00 for everyone else. What is included in the ticket price: 2 free beers at the last stop on your adventure, supplies and materials needed for your quest and a little surprise at the end ;)
