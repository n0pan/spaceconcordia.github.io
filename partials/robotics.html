ROBOTICS DIVISION


This division came in existence as a result of a big capstone project. Over the course of a year, we designed and built a rover for the University Rover Competition in Utah. The rover is supposed to function in a simulated Mars environment. The Robotics Division also gets to work on other robotic projects. This year, we're planning to send a team to IEEE's RoboWars competition. It has 4 subteams: Software, Power, Electrical, and Mechanical.


Software team: works on the rover communication on the software end, the interface the controllers will use to interact with the rover, and the video streaming


Electrical team: works on the microcontrollers and logic circuitry.


Power team: works with the electrical power distribution system and the battery supply.


Mechanical team: deals with the rover's physical structure, component layout, specifications for desired tasks, and the machining of parts and adjustments. We are currently recruiting Mechanical and Electrical Engineers.
